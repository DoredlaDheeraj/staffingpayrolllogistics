Payroll Services

Payroll Processing

Accurate calculation of employee salaries, taxes, deductions, and benefits.Includes payslip generation and timely salary disbursement.

Statutory Compliance

Ensures adherence to labor laws like PF, ESI, TDS, and professional tax.Helps avoid legal penalties and maintains organizational compliance.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)

End-to-end recruitment handled by an external expert team.Includes sourcing, screening, interviewing, and onboarding candidates.

Delegation of HR functions like payroll, employee records, and benefits to a third-party.Improves efficiency and allows businesses to focus on core operations.
